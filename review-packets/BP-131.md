## Verification

Neon table listing returned all 15 expected public tables.

Table-count query:

```sql
SELECT COUNT(*)::int AS builder_table_count
FROM information_schema.tables
WHERE table_schema = 'public'
  AND table_name IN (...15 builder tables...);
```

Result:

```text
builder_table_count = 15
```

Foreign-key verification found the expected references to `builder_tasks`, including the
`builder_tasks.parent_task_id` self-reference. The FK-free tables remain FK-free:
`builder_opus_log`, `builder_chains`, `builder_agent_profiles`, `pool_state`, `async_jobs`,
and `builder_assumptions`.
